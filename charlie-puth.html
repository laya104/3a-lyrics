Nine Track Mind is by far one of Charlie Puth's most popular albums.
It was released on January 29, 2016 by Atlantic Records, and the album
reached number 6 on the Billboard 200. Nine Track Mind consists of 12
songs. In his finalized track, Puth replaced the tracks, "Know Your Name"
and "Hard," with "Dangerously" and "We Don't Talk Anymore." His three
singles, "Marvin Gaye," "One Call Away," and "We Don't Talk Anymore," topped the charts.
